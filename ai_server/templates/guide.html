How to Generate Terrains

Basic Inputs

Set the basic dimensions and parameters of your terrain. Provide:

  • Width & Height: Between 512 to 1024 units.
  • Depth: Maximum height of the terrain, between 65 and 200 units.
  • Octaves: Levels of detail, between 1 and 15.
  • Scale: Adjust the size of features, from 70 to 500 units.

Describing Your Terrain

Use descriptive terms to specify the terrain type:

  • Desert: No vegetation unless explicitly added.
  • Mountain: High elevation with rocky or snowy textures.
  • Forest: Dense with trees and grass.
  • Snow: Only snow textures unless other textures are added.

Adding Water & Atmosphere

Customize water features and the environment:

  • Water Types: Choose from river, lake, ocean, or none.
  • Time of Day: Set from 0 (midnight) to 24 (midnight).
  • Fog Intensity: Control between 0 (no fog) to 0.5 (dense fog).
  • Sky Tint: Provide RGB values for sky colors.
